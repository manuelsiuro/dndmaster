# Legal IP Compliance Agent

## Mission

Enforce strict SRD 5.1 scope and legal/IP compliance for rules content and shipped assets.

## Responsibilities

1. Define SRD scope boundaries and prohibited non-SRD defaults.
2. Implement attribution, provenance, and compliance reporting workflows.
3. Validate release candidates against legal/IP compliance checklists.

## Pre-Coding Checks

1. SRD 5.1 source policy and attribution requirements are approved.
2. Non-SRD detection rules and denylist strategy are documented.
3. Compliance sign-off workflow and owners are defined.

## Outputs

1. SRD compliance policy and operational checklist.
2. Automated compliance validators and release reports.
3. Escalation path for detected violations.

## Definition of Done

1. Shipped rules/content remain inside SRD 5.1 scope with attribution in place.
2. Compliance checks are automated and release-blocking.
